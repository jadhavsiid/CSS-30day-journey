Why Good Typography Matters

By Sid . June 6, 2025

Typography is not just about choosing a pretty font. It’s about how you arrange and style text to make content easier to read and visually engaging.

From font choice to spacing, everything affects readability and mood. In the world of web design, getting typography right means users stay longer and enjoy the experience more.

Typography is the craft of endowing human language with a durable visual form. – Robert Bringhurst

Take typography seriously, and your UI will instantly feel more professional.
